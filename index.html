WELCOME!!

CHIREC International is a K-12 school located in Hyderabad, India. The school was founded in 1989 by Mrs. Ratna D. Reddy.

ABOUT US

Established in 1989 by Ratna Reddy, CHIREC was founded as a vibrant summer camp for children to develop their skills in arts, sports, and technology. Over the last 30 years, CHIREC International School has grown to become a leader in K-12 education as an institution that instills a passion for learning and innovation. CHIREC International School offers CBSE, IB and Cambridge curricula, and is recognized as one of the best schools in Hyderabad, Telangana and in India.
